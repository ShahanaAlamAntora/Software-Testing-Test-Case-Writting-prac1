How to write the test cases for bank application

  1. Bank Admin functionality

  • Admin login ( With Valid and Invalid)
  • Available of  all home links for Admin role
  • Admin change password (Valid and Invalid)
  • Admin logout successfully

  1. Bank Branch functionality

  • Create new branch ( With Valid and Invalid)
  • Create new branch duplicate branch
  • Update branch details(Valid and Invalid)
  • Verify branch search option

  1. Opening new bank account

  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Photo
  • Address proof
  • Identity proof
  • Introducers
  • PAN card
  • Minimum deposit
  • Facility Required (checkbook/ATM card/Online banking)
  • Customer Signature

  1. Bank Transfer functionality

  • Verify for secured URL (HTTPS) and  authenticity
  • Validate for username and password
  • Verify for SMS authentication after login
  • Verify for dashboard
  • Verify for online money transfer feature
  • Check if the transfer funds during the specific period(9 am to 5 pm)
  • Verify for adding the beneficiary for NEFT or RTGS
  • Verify for beneficiary name, IFSC code, bank name, fund amount and purpose of the transfer
  • Verify for 2-step authentication
  • Verify interbank fund transfer
  • Verify for funds transferred between the in-business hours
  • Verify for funds transferred between the off-business hours
  • Verify for notification is sent to the sender and receiver
  • Verify the amount transfer and debit
